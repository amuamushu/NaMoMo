Our Story

NaMoMo is a sustainability web app that allows users to track their household energy usage and reduce their ecological footprint, in addition to saving money. NaMoMo was inspired by the group's understanding of the importance of environmental protection and their experiences with dreadful and plain energy bills. Each month when the electricity company sends the bill, it also sends a breakdown showing what appliances were most use, and how it compares to the last month. However, it's the 21st century, and we thought: "What better way to do this than to build an app that lets you take control of that information?" It is our hope that we can partner with electricity companies in order to recieve the usage data from the source and, in turn, show people in real-time what their electricity usage is.
